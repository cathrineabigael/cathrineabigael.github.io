SaaS Point of Sales System

A Software-based point-of-sales system that can help companies record and process transactions accurately without having to have experience in the business field. This software as a service provides a simple and inexpensive maintenance and upgrade solution (compared to a system built specifically for a single company) because it is performed by the service provider. This system also provides an opportunity for companies to configure as needed. (Developed using Laravel, made to meet the graduation requirements of a Bachelor's Degree (S1))
